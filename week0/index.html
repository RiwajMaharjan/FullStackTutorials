The Secret Behind Perfectly Fluffy Pancakes

There’s nothing quite like waking up to a stack of warm, fluffy pancakes. After trying countless variations, I’ve learned that the secret lies in two simple techniques: mixing the batter gently and letting it rest. Overmixing makes the pancakes dense, but giving the batter a few minutes to settle allows the ingredients to blend naturally, creating that light, airy texture. Whether you enjoy them with fresh fruits, honey, or classic maple syrup, mastering this basic recipe will turn every breakfast into something special.
